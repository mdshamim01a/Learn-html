solo series

Solos is an American dramatic anthology television miniseries created by David Weil and produced by Amazon Studios. It stars Morgan Freeman, Anne Hathaway, Helen Mirren, Uzo Aduba, Anthony Mackie, Constance Wu, Dan Stevens and Nicole Beharie. The seven-episode series premiered on May 21, 2021 on Amazon Prime Video in UK, United States, Australia, Canada, Ireland, India, New Zealand and later that year in additional territories

fictional character biography

A fictional character biography, or character bio, is a detailed overview of a fictional person's life, covering everything from their name and appearance to their personality, backstory, and relationships. It can be used by authors and role-players to develop a character, ensure consistency, and help others understand the character before a narrative begins. A full-fledged character biography might delve into their history, motivations, and traits, while a shorter version used for introductions focuses on key descriptive details.
